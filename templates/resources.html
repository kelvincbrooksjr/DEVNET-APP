Resources

Cisco DevNet Info

A lot of the information I used for obtaining my DevNet Associate came from https://developer.cisco.com/ The rest came for Pluralsight, courses by Nick Russo, and CBT Nuggets, courses by Knox Hutchinson and Ben Finkel. The DevNet sight contains many sandboxes for free pratice. Here is a list of tools and programs I recommend you install to use to practice fully: Python, Docker, VirtualBox/VMWare or WSL2 if on Windows (Some things work more consistently in Linux), Postman, VS Code or Sublime Text, and Git Bash (once again, if using Windows).

AWS Solutions Architect Associate

A lot of the information I used for obtaining my AWSCSAA came from FreeCodeCamp. There is a video by Andrew Brown which is amazing. Other great resources I used include: Pluralsight, CBT Nuggets, and Amazon's free training material. It is extremely easy to start up an AWS account. AWS offers numerous resources that are "free tier". Note: free tier will not be production level for a mid-size company or larger. However, it's perfect for getting your hands dirty and getting intimate with many common services which is paramount for success on the examination.

Juniper Certs are free

As of right now Juniper associate level certifications can be free. There are 5 Juniper associate level certifications: JNICA-Junos, JNCIA-Sec, JNCIA-Cloud, JNCIA-DevOps, and JNCDA (Design). You can look into this be going to https://learningportal.juniper.net/ So far, I have studied for and acheived the JNICA-Junos cert. The information on Juniper's site is ample. Their practice tests are great. After taking the training there is a voucher examination, passing the voucher exam will result in being sent a voucher to take the examination for free.
